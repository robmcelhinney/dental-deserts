Overlay reading guide

Choropleth colors are area-level context, not appointment availability at a clinic.

Methodology and interpretation

“Accepting NHS adults/children” is self-reported status from source feeds and can be stale.

It does not guarantee immediate appointments, wait times, or capacity.

Access pressure = practices per 10,000 residents in an MSOA.

Dental desert risk is a composite of low practice density, low adult-accepting density, and deprivation.
